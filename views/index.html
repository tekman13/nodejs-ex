Our Vision

To provide seamless and exceptional enterprise-wide and center-specific services with a customer-centric and innovation-focused approach in support of FDA's mission to promote and protect public health.

Our Mission

The Office of Enterprise Management Services (OEMS) provides strategic and tactical enterprise-wide services through the development and implementation of administrative policies, programs, and initiatives. Concurrently, OEMS manages day-to-day center-specific operations related to human capital, resource management, information governance, compliance and conflict prevention, and other operational support activities across the Office of the Commissioner and Office of Operations.
